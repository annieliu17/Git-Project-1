Dogs

h1>

Dogs are very friendly animals.

Dogs are domesticated mammals and are not natural wild animals. They were originally bred from wolves, but today, they play different roles. Some dogs are used as pets, while others are used to help humans do work such as police dogs. They are good pets because they are usually playful, friendly, loyal and listen to humans.

A medium-sized dog has an average weight of around 35 to 65 pounds. They are of all different colors, but there are no dogs in the wild because dogs were human-bred from wolves.
